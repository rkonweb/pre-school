Common Questions

Everything you want to know

How long does setup take?
Most schools are fully live within 5 minutes of signing up. Your dashboard is pre-configured with sensible defaults. Our onboarding team helps with data migration and staff training at no extra cost.
Can I use only some modules?
Yes. Every module is independently usable. Most schools start with Admissions and Parents, then add Billing and Curriculum as they grow. You only pay for what you use.
Does it work for multiple branches?
Absolutely. Bodhiboard is built for chains and franchises. You get one central dashboard to manage all branches, standardised curriculum, and consolidated reporting — with branch-level granularity when you need it.
Is my school's data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're hosted on AWS with 99.9% uptime SLA. Your data is never shared or used to train models. You own it entirely.
What does the free trial include?
Your 30-day trial includes full access to all modules with no student limit. No credit card required. At the end, you choose which plan fits — or simply stop with no charge.
What kind of support is available?
Every plan includes email and WhatsApp support. Growth and Institution plans include a dedicated success manager. We also have a Help Centre, video library and weekly live Q&A sessions.
