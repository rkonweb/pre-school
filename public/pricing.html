Pricing FAQ

Questions about pricing

What's included in the 30-day free trial?
Your trial includes full access to all modules in the Growth plan with no student limits and no credit card required. After 30 days, choose a plan — or stop with no charge.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades apply immediately. Downgrades take effect at end of billing cycle. We always prorate unused time.
How does annual billing work?
Annual plans are billed once per year and save you 25%. You receive a single invoice. If you're unhappy within the first 60 days, we'll refund you in full.
Is there a setup or onboarding fee?
No. Setup is free for all plans. Growth includes a guided onboarding session at no extra cost. Institution plans include full white-glove onboarding as part of the custom package.
What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, credit cards, debit cards and bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS). Institution clients can also pay by cheque or purchase order. GST invoices issued automatically.
What happens to our data if we cancel?
Your data belongs to you. Export everything as CSV files. We retain your data for 90 days after cancellation so you can return if you change your mind.
